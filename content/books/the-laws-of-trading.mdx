A simple introduction to the principles of trading. Provides mental tools useful for all investing and decision making. Each chapter covers a new important concept: models, edge, liquidity, cost, capacity, risk, etc. Many real world examples to build intuitions. Probably essential for anyone who hasn't already worked at a hedge fund or trading desk of some sort.
